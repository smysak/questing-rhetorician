Rhetorician's Quest

You are a budding young rhetorician. Armed with the power of rhetoric, the world and all its glories lie before you. Will you take up the quest?

Behold, your way is stymied by a myriad of peasants, each involved in their own affairs. To achieve power, you must choose your words carefully, and bind them to your will.

Some peasants are succeptible to an appeal to personal credibility.

Some peasants are succeptible to an appeal to emotional manipulation.

Some peasants are only succeptible to logical appeals.

Make sure you make rhetorical choices carefully, according to your audience (i.e. the peasant in question).
